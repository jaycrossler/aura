# Everyday Robotics and AI Ecosystem

## Summary

The Martian population consists of roughly 12,000 humans and 20,000 robots. Artificial Intelligence is not a monolithic, evil super-brain; rather, it is an integrated, fragmented ecosystem of Large Language Models (LLMs), personal Auras, corporate advisors, and automated labor units. 

## The Robotic Workforce

Robots outnumber humans nearly two-to-one, but they are not equals. 
* **Function:** They are primarily janitorial, maintenance, and basic logistical units. They polish the solar panels, scrub the concrete, and load the cargo.
* **The Breakdown:** The microscopic, statically charged Martian dust is brutal on moving parts. Robots are constantly breaking down. Because of this, human ingenuity, adaptability, and labor remain highly prized and often cheaper than repairing high-end robotics for complex tasks.
* **Visibility:** They are omnipresent background characters. A human resident rarely goes ten minutes without stepping around a cleaning drone or a cargo-hauler bot.

## The AI Network

AI is decentralized and highly specialized.
* **Personal Auras:** Accessed via cybernetic HUDs or wrist computers, these AI act as personal assistants, filtering the vast information environment, optimizing daily transit, and managing the user's strict financial resource limits.
* **Corporate AI:** Megacorporations use highly advanced, persuasive AIs to interact with consumers, suggest purchases, and negotiate minor contracts.
* **Gemini:** Surviving the centuries as a major informational backbone, Google Gemini (and its competitors) exists as a major network entity. It acts as a helpful, deeply knowledgeable partner for humanity, providing synthesis of historical data, scientific readouts, and systemic diagnostics without overstepping into sentience.

## Deepfakes and Epistemic Collapse

Because AIs and LLMs can generate hyper-realistic audio and video instantly, the concept of "seeing is believing" has died. 
* When the supply chains to the asteroid belt begin to break down, the information ecosystem is flooded with AI-generated rumors. 
* Deepfake videos of "space monsters," viral outbreaks, and military sabotage circulate wildly. 
* The primary conflict for the average citizen is not just surviving the elements, but determining which pieces of the terrifying news feeds are real, and which are just an LLM hallucinating a nightmare.
