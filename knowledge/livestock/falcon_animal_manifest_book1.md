# Falcon Animal Manifest — Book 1 Voyage

## Summary

Total aboard: **58 females, 2 males** (Jace and Cerberus).

The "fifty-eight women" figure Jace references in Chapter 1 counts:
Maureen + Mei + Lanchee + Siren + 54 livestock females = 58.
Cerberus is the other male. Jace and Cerberus are the only males on the ship.

No settlers aboard this voyage. All life support capacity allocated to livestock.

---

## Container Mapping

Following the *Falcon*'s revised berth system (36 standardized 13m module berths —
see `ships/ship_falcon.md`), the 10 livestock enclosures are mapped onto **5 module
berths**. Each container splits into two ~6m pens divided by a shared central access
corridor.

| Container | Pens | Pairing Logic |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Sheep (01) + Goats (05) | Both flock ruminants; similar climate and feed needs |
| 2 | Pigs (02) + Cattle (06) | Both larger-bodied; heavier-duty shared corridor |
| 3 | Ducks (03) + Geese (09) | Both waterfowl; shared moisture-handling environmental system |
| 4 | Chickens (04) + Turkeys (07) | Both poultry; shared dust-handling system |
| 5 | Rabbits (08) + Vacant/Utility (10) | Quiet module; rabbits only |

## Plumbing Crisis — Iced Berths (Resolved)

The Ch. 2 plumbing crisis ices over two berths on one arc of the ring.

- **Pigs (02) & Heifers (06):** Share Container 2, which is iced — trapping the
  heifers alongside the pigs; raises stakes.
- **Siren-in-danger:** Resolved. Siren has no assigned container (Container 5 has
  rabbits and vacant utility pen). Jace panics during the crisis because Siren
  habitually hangs out in the Duck container (03), and he fears she is trapped.
  This preserves dramatic stakes and gives Maureen reason to let Jace attempt repair.
- **Rodents/Small animals:** Rabbits (08) represent "small animals" referenced in scene.
