# Juno Station

## Summary

Juno Station is anchored to 3 Juno, an S-type (stony-silicaceous) asteroid in the inner belt. S-types are among the most common asteroid types and are valuable for silicate minerals, olivine, and metallic iron-nickel. Juno Station is an established inner-belt mining operation with a particular focus on construction-grade materials.

Juno's proximity to Fortuna (both inner-belt) makes the two stations natural trading partners — Fortuna supplies food and biological materials; Juno supplies construction minerals and specialty silicates. Inter-station transits between the two, while still weeks-long depending on relative orbital positions, are among the shorter belt hops.
