# Elysium

## Summary

Elysium is the cosmopolitan, international crown jewel of Mars. Driven by European and Arabian "Old Money," it operates as a heavily regulated, neutral business hub. It sits a two-hour surface train ride away from Terminus (when the tracks aren't buried by dust storms), with the corridor between them acting as a massive stretch of cargo and shuttle zones.

## Culture and Economy

* **The Las Vegas of Mars:** Elysium pulses with the energy of high-stakes trade. It is the place where mega-corporations (like Origin Industries) broker massive logistics deals, secure asteroid mining rights, and trade fortunes. 
* **The Rules:** The city is notorious for its bureaucracy. There is a saying that to buy a cup of coffee in Elysium, you have to follow a thousand rules, all of which are displayed on your HUD. Lawyers and corporate negotiators are the elite class here.
* **Aesthetics:** In stark contrast to the utilitarian concrete of the military bases or the grime of Pangu, Elysium features grand, sweeping architectural designs. It relies heavily on premium, imported Earth aesthetics, making it a playground for the wealthy and the ambitious.
