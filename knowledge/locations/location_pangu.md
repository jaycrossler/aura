# Pangu

## Summary

Pangu is the industrial powerhouse of Mars, managed by Chinese state and corporate interests. Located far from the equator in the prime mining territories near the Tharsis volcanoes, Pangu is an isolated, gritty, and relentlessly productive city. 

## Atmosphere and Environment

If Terminus is the commercial storefront of Mars, Pangu is the boiler room. 
* **The Grime:** The city is notoriously dirty. The ambient pollution from fuel refinement and heavy mineral extraction is so pervasive that visitors claim you can't order a cup of coffee in Pangu without finding a thin, iridescent film of greasy jet fuel floating on the top.
* **Labor:** Rumors constantly swirl in Terminus and Elysium about the labor conditions in Pangu. Fearmongering suggests forced labor or brutal quotas, though the reality is obscured by state secrecy and the physical distance between the cities.
* **Function:** Pangu is essential to the Martian economy. It produces the lion's share of the refined fuel and heavy metals required to keep the nuclear ferries and orbital catch networks operational. Without Pangu, the logistics chain to the asteroid belt would collapse.
