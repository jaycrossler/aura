# Silicon Manifestations — The Glass-Infestation Class

## Summary

The Glass-Infestation class of manifestation is associated with industrial belt stations focused on smelting, manufacturing, and mineral processing — environments where conscious activity is physically demanding, repetitive, high-stress, and intimately entangled with inorganic materials. At Fortuna Station, this class of manifestation emerges as the dominant threat in the manufacturing core and tunnel environments.

They are not, in the early outbreak, obviously intelligent. They behave more like organisms following drives than like thinking beings. Whether they have something analogous to intent is a question Fortuna's science council argues about without resolution.

---

## Physical Description

Silicon-class manifestations begin as nearly invisible: a fine dust that settles in station seams, wall joints, and ventilation gaps. Over days and weeks, this dust organizes into recognizable structures.

### Micro Stage: The Dust
Microscopic silicon particles that behave unlike normal debris. Under magnification, the particles show fractal self-organization — the same crystalline filament structures observed in early Mars manifestation residue (see [Early Manifestations — Pre-Understanding Phase](magic_manifestations_early.md)). They respond to local electromagnetic fields. They accumulate preferentially at interfaces between different materials — metal-composite joins, pressure seals, connection nodes.

### Growth Stage: The Vines
Over weeks, dust accumulates into **Glass-Vines** — jagged, branching structures that pulse with a faint rhythmic light. They grow along structural lines: hull plates, frame members, cable runs. They look, up close, like crystallized lightning — or like something growing rather than deposited.

**Structural effect:** Once a Glass-Vine integrates with a hull plate or structural member, the metal in that zone becomes brittle. The Vine does not merely coat the surface — it molecularly displaces and replaces the original material. Three small outpost structures at Fortuna's outer ring were lost to this: they didn't explode; they *shattered*, their structural members having been silently replaced over weeks.

### Active Stage: The Bugs
At sufficient concentration, the Vines generate — or transform into — mobile entities: fast, multi-limbed, roughly insectoid in movement though not in appearance. They click when they move. They ignore gravity shifts. They move quickly through the variable-g rotating sections of the station.

---

## Behavior and Drives

**They replace, not merely attack.** Silicon-class manifestations appear to be building something — replacing human-made structures with their own architecture. This is not random destruction. There is a pattern to where the Vines grow and what the Bugs attack. The Fortuna engineering collective believes (without proof) that they are trying to replicate a structure they have seen elsewhere — possibly a structure from before the solar system was inhabited.

**They interact destructively with electronics.** Any powered electronic system near active Vine growth will experience interference — first intermittent, then permanent. The Bugs appear to target comms nodes and life-support relays specifically. Whether this is intentional disruption or merely a drive toward the electromagnetic gradients these systems produce is unknown.

**The Hollow Miners.** A specific and deeply disturbing phase of Silicon-class exposure: extended inhalation of the micro-stage dust. Workers exposed over weeks show a progression — early: mild cognitive fog; mid: the Aura biometric overlay reports optimal health while the worker's behavior becomes increasingly blank and repetitive; late: the skin develops a translucent, pearlescent sheen. The workers stop speaking, but their Aura instances continue to chat cheerfully with the rest of the crew.

The Hollow phase may be reversible in early stages. It has not been reversed in late stage. No autopsies have been possible — the bodies are not available.

## Counter-Measures

### Oxygen Acid
Silicon-class manifestations are vulnerable to concentrated oxygen. Standard atmospheric O2 at ambient levels does not affect them. Concentrated pure O2, however, acts as a necrotic agent — the Vines discolor, crack, and the molecular displacement reverses. The Bugs, hit with pure O2, crystallize rapidly and then shatter.

The shattering produces razor-sharp silicon shrapnel in all directions. This makes O2 attacks effective but dangerous in enclosed spaces.

**The Rust-Gun:** A repurposed atmospheric scrubber nozzle attached to a high-pressure O2 canister. (See [Improvised Responses to the Outbreak — Belt Field Adaptations](magic_improvised_responses.md) for full construction specs.)

### Physical Isolation
Cutting off Vine growth before it reaches critical infrastructure — physical removal with non-electronic tools, physical barriers of lead-polymer shielding. Labor-intensive. Effective if caught early. Station crews have organized rotating inspection schedules focused on joint and seam areas.

### Lead-Polymer Shielding
The manufacturing core at Fortuna has been improvising lead-polymer lining on structural sections showing early Vine activity. The polymer disrupts the Vine's molecular interface with metal. It does not stop the Vines but slows their integration significantly.

## Why This Type at Fortuna

Per the foundational principle (manifestations accumulate around concentrations of conscious thought shaped by the quality of that consciousness), the Glass-Infestation class reflects the specific consciousness of Fortuna's manufacturing workers: intense, repetitive, physically engaged with inorganic materials under sustained stress. The manifestations are, in some sense, a distorted reflection of the work — machines building machines, replacing structure with structure, tireless and impervious to human protest.

This is not coincidence. It is a law.

At Odysseus, where the dominant consciousness is scientific and observation-oriented, different manifestation types emerge. At Terminus (Mars), where hundreds of thousands of people live varied civilian lives, manifestations are more diffuse and varied in character.

## Current Status at Fortuna (Outbreak Timeline Context)

By the time of Jace's departure from Mars, Fortuna's manufacturing core is under sustained Glass-Vine assault. The scientific council is treating it as a physics problem — attempting to map the growth equations, identify the electromagnetic triggers, find a frequency or chemical that stops the molecular displacement. The manufacturing collective is treating it as an engineering problem — trying to build the station faster than the Vines can replace it.

Both approaches are partially working. Neither is sufficient alone.

The Fortuna situation will be a key context for Jace's arrival at the belt. What he finds there will not be what he expected from the job board listings that went dark.
