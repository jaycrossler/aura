# Master Timeline

A high-level chronology of the events that shape the story's world. Specific events should eventually have their own files in `/timeline/event_*.md` and be cross-referenced here.

Dates are working figures and should be treated as adjustable.

---

## Deep Prehistory (~3000-4000 years before story start)

- The Veil (a planetary nebula or molecular cloud) drifts into the line of sight between Earth and a supernova approximately 100 light-years distant.
- Resonance flux from the supernova fades from Earth.
- Pre-Veil magical traditions, having previously functioned with measurable effect, lose efficacy over centuries.
- Surviving traditions persist as religion, folklore, esotericism, and myth.

## The 21st Century

### 2040s — First Footprints
- **2040.** First crewed Mars landing. Flags-and-footprints era.
- **2042-2048.** Initial science missions, return-trip-only.
- **2049.** First permanent base established (small, rotating crew).

### 2050s — Bootstrapping
- ISRU propellant production matures on Mars.
- Reusable heavy-lift drives Earth-orbit launch costs down by an order of magnitude.
- First continuous Mars presence.
- **Aura precursor systems** emerge from open-weights AI movement.

### 2060s — Settlement
- Mars population grows from dozens to a few hundred.
- First colony-class missions deliver permanent settlers.
- Commercial space firms begin to consolidate; early megacorp formation.
- First Mars-born infants.
- Aura architecture standardizes; widespread adoption begins.

### 2070s — Expansion
- Mars population in the low thousands.
- First cycler ship (***Niña***) commissioned.
- U.S. military presence on Mars begins.
- Chinese national space program establishes permanent Mars settlement.
- Cybernetic augmentation moves from medical prosthetic to broader augmentation category.

### 2080s — Maturation
- Mars population in the several thousands.
- Second cycler (***Pinta***) enters service.
- Indian-led multinational Mars settlement founded.
- First recognizable Mars-born adult generation reaches majority.
- U.S. statehood debate for Mars begins in Congress.

### 2090s — Statehood and Industrial Maturity
- Mars population crosses 10,000.
- Third cycler (***Santa María***) enters service. Cycler fleet at full strength.
- **The United States of Mars admitted as the 56th state of the U.S.**
- Substantial U.S. military presence on Mars.
- First nuclear-thrust ferries begin running from Mars to the asteroid belt.
- Asteroid belt prospecting accelerates; first permanent belter outposts.
- **Juno Station** (3 Juno) founded (~2097).
- **Psyche Station** (16 Psyche) founded (~2098).

## The Early 22nd Century

### 2100-2104 — Belt Expansion
- **Fortuna Station** (3 Fortuna) founded (~2099) and enters growth phase.
- **Sylvia Station** (87 Sylvia) founded (~2100).
- **Odysseus Station** (143 Odysseus, Jupiter L4) founded (~2101) — farthest permanent human habitation.
- Belt population grows from hundreds to low thousands across the five stations.
- Mars-belt ferry traffic reaches mature operational tempo.
- Hub-and-spoke model develops: each station becomes infrastructure hub for surrounding corporate/national worker ships.
- Ground-based astronomical anomalies begin to be noticed at outer-system posts.
- Odysseus Station, at ~5.2 AU, begins experiencing first anomalous events as resonance flux reaches Jupiter trojan space.
- Cybernetic implants enter widespread military and corporate use.
- **Jace Grant** recruited into Air Force ROTC; accumulates college debt.

### ~2103 — Jace's Career
- Jace Grant commissions as a second lieutenant, specializes in drone command and control.
- Volunteers for Mars Detachment; selected after competitive process.

### 2104 — Jace's Voyage to Mars
- Jace undergoes HUD implant surgery.
- Jace travels to Mars aboard the ***Pinta*** — four months, crowded, uncomfortable, spent cramming drone operations courses and recovering from surgery.

### 2105 — Story Present (Working Date)

**Early 2105 — Mars Arrival Arc:**
- Jace arrives on Mars. Tours drone control center. Notices Mei and Mr. Sylvester in secure meeting with base commander. Belt anomalies mentioned as background concern.
- Jace video-calls family: Dawn and Chris in Virginia, sister Athena at college. Failed call to Artemis at Odysseus Station.
- Jace's medical checkout. Genetic condition found. Honorable discharge. HUD deactivated. 1 million credits debt, no return berth, 30 days to vacate.
- **Jace's hard months**: menial labor (sewers, gear repair), financial desperation. Parents offer to sell house; refused. Artemis sends 50,000 credits and a package-delivery errand.
- Jace delivers package to the Smuggler at Mars launch facility. Relationship established.
- Emergency dropout on Origin belt run. Smuggler facilitates Jace's slot and crash animal-handling certification.
- Jace encounters Mei during certification. Later: Mei and Mr. Sylvester recruit Jace. Intel deal: assistance for HUD reactivation and payments.

**Mid 2105 — Departure:**
- Jace boards the ***Falcon*** (Barnyard 1) bound for Fortuna Station. 28 settlers, 7 crew, several hundred animals.
- Family farewell calls. Brief, degraded contact with Artemis — she sounds different; Jace can't place why.
- Falcon departs Mars. Nuclear burn. The pig-cam era begins.

**Mid-2105 to Late-2105 — The Transit:**
- Four-and-a-half months aboard the Falcon.
- Weeks 1-3: Jace adjusts, overwhelmed.
- Weeks 4-7: Jace becomes competent; bonds with animals.
- Weeks 8-12: Anomalies begin. Animal behavior, sensor disagreements, Aura flags.
- Weeks 13-17: Anomalies daily. The *Falcon*'s Aura logs "resonance interference."
- Late transit: pressure scare, near-emergency, unexplained cause.
- Communications with Earth/Mars degrading throughout. Belt comms network becomes the only reliable link.

**Late 2105 — Arrival and Climax:**
- Jace arrives at Fortuna Station. Blackout imposed or tightening.
- Station receives the animals. Pig-cams go live. Belt entertainment culture around livestock begins.
- First unambiguous magical event Marcus witnesses (or causes). Book 1 ends.

### 2105-2115 — The Belt Era of the Returning Arts
- Belt population grows; cross-station cooperation deepens; belter political identity solidifies.
- Odysseus Station develops most advanced magical practice — isolated, highest flux, developing independently.
- First synthesis schools of the Returning Arts emerge from cross-station practitioner networks.
- Earth and Mars conduct increasingly desperate research as the radiation front continues toward them.
- Various corporate and governmental attempts to penetrate the blackout.

### 2115-2125 — The Front Reaches Mars
- Resonance flux reaches Mars in measurable intensity. First Mars sensitives manifest.
- Communications blackout partially lifted as belt-Mars contact resumes on belter terms.

### 2125-2135 — The Front Reaches Earth
- Resonance flux reaches Earth.
- The world transforms. (Series-long arc.)
- The Grant family on Earth (Dawn, Chris, Athena) experience a world beginning to change.
