### Scene 02 — Day 1: Arrival (Chapter 01)

Spatial flow: Pinta → terminal → biometric scan → rover → walking tour (Coffee Tree Dome, Bird Dome) → base overview → billet → sleep.

#### Scene 02a: Shuttle Descent and Biometric Scan
- **Chapter:** Ch01
- **Date:** Day 1, ~2105-03-15 [STORYBOT anchor]
- **Time:** ~02:00
- **Major beats:**
  - Pinta lands, Jace disembarks
  - Rich is at the terminal carrying a standard travel mug (his normal mug — small)
  - Rich produces a hardened box, has Jace place his palm on it

#### Scene 02b: Rover Tour
- **Chapter:** Ch01
- **Time:** ~02:30 → ~13:00 (terminal processing, walking tour, rover ride)
- **Major beats:**
  - WiFi excitement dialogue and download-queue exchange
  - Hair-length convention explained (levy, military stipend, Jace is currently paying 40 credits/month)
  - Rotator mentioned in passing: 40 people going home next week, twice normal
  - Walking tour: Coffee Tree Dome (Rich's favorite, different atmospheric conditions, Mars coffee sourcing)
  - Walking tour: Bird Dome (100-credit entry, higher pressure, fake lake, goslings, Mars Goose Cam)
  - Rover ride to base perimeter, HUD/fiscal conversation, entertainment + blush sub-beat
  - Pangu/Pengu reference (oily-clothed workers transferring through the dome)
  - Lunch

**Rich's coffee:** Standard travel mug throughout Day 1. Warm, professional, one refill.

#### Scene 02c: Base Overview (Day 1 Afternoon)
- **Time:** ~14:00–17:00
- **Major beats:**

  - Brief intro to the base (Space Force vs. Air Force service rivalry, uniform colors noted)

**Note:** The drone center tour does NOT happen on Day 1. It happens on Day 3 when Jace formally reports for duty.

**Time gap to next scene:** ~1 day (Day 2 acclimation)

## StoryBot Continuity Rules

1. Verify character state matches scene start state
2. Track knowledge progression — Jace cannot know things before they happen
3. Honor time gaps — "yesterday" must be accurate
4. Emotional carry-over — discharge bleeds into all subsequent scenes
5. Physical consistency — Jace's weight, HUD state, hair
6. Relationship state — Mei "first meeting" must square with prior glimpses
7. Address conventions — Rich calls Jace "Jace"; junior enlisted call him "LT"
8. Family anchors — Jelly and Jinx exist; their wellbeing is load-bearing for the horse farm offer
9. Cerberus pre-bond — weeks of breeder visits precede the Origin negotiation
10. Rich's coffee — starts small, grows with each encounter
11. MilX partitions — Military OS and Civilian OS are separate; civilian partition always active; Military OS deactivated at discharge; Level 1 re-enabled by Sylvester
12. Fake video ubiquity — real manifestation footage is dismissed as synthetic; this is structural, not incidental
