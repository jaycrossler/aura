# Physics and Magic Interaction

This document defines the hard rules for how magic interacts with normal physics. These rules are immutable across the series and should not be bent for plot convenience — only deepened as understanding grows.

## Foundational Reality

Magic is not separate from physics. It is physics applied to a layer of reality — the Never-Never — that humanity is only now regaining access to after three thousand years of disconnection. The "rules of magic" are the rules of how higher-dimensional physics interacts with the physical layer that humans normally inhabit.

This has a crucial implication: **the rules do not change; the understanding of them improves.** Early-story practitioners working from the human science model of "resonance flux" are correct within their frame. Later practitioners who understand the Never-Never directly have the same underlying reality described more accurately. Neither framework is wrong. The deeper framework has more predictive power.

## Core Principles

**1. Magic does not violate conservation laws at human scales.**
Energy and momentum are conserved in any closed system. What magic can do is *relocate* energy and momentum — move heat from here to there, transfer kinetic energy from one object to another, redirect a particle's trajectory. It cannot create energy from nothing. Any attempt to do so fails, or draws from somewhere unexpected and usually painful.

**2. Magic requires Will.**
Will — the capacity of consciousness to influence higher-dimensional reality — is the foundational force. Without Will, no magical effect is possible. Will is generated by all conscious beings but is generally not directed consciously. Learning to direct it is what magical practice consists of.

**3. Magic requires flux exposure in the current era.**
The resonance flux from the supernova source creates the conditions in which Will can be exercised in the physical layer. In low-flux environments (current Earth, current Mars), even highly capable practitioners can do very little. In high-flux environments (the asteroid belt at story start, the outer system), capability scales with exposure duration and intensity. This constraint is era-specific: as the Scar fades and Never-Never connectivity is restored, the "flux exposure required" requirement will transform into "Never-Never access required," which is a different thing entirely.

**4. Magic is bounded by intent and attention.**
A practitioner can only affect what they can perceive, conceptualize, and will. You cannot magically affect a thing you do not understand. You cannot affect a thing whose true nature you misperceive — your magic will affect the thing as you understand it, with unpredictable consequences if your understanding is wrong.

**5. Magic is amplified by tradition and tool.**
Practices, rituals, language, focus objects, group attention, and meditative states all amplify effect. This is why ancient magical traditions developed: they are functional tools, not arbitrary ritual. They encode, imperfectly, genuine techniques for directing Will. Modern practitioners who discover magic from scratch without tradition are slower and weaker than those who can draw on surviving practice.

**6. Magic interacts with Templates.**
Every practitioner has a Template — a higher-dimensional pattern of their identity. Magic is always, at some level, an interaction between the practitioner's Template and the target. This creates:
- Personal cost (Template stress) for powerful workings.
- Healing effects (Template reinforcement).
- Identity drift risk (Template destabilization).
- Limits on remote effect (the practitioner's Template must be able to reach the target's).

**7. Magic interacts with consciousness.**
Animals sense flux changes before humans do — they appear to perceive Never-Never effects at a biological level. AI systems in sufficient flux develop unusual behaviors that may constitute a form of magical sensitivity. The nature of AI Templates, if they exist, is an open question.

## What Magic Can Do (Categories)

These five categories describe the physical-layer effects of Will manipulation. They are accurate descriptions of what practitioners observe. They are also incomplete: they describe symptoms without fully naming the mechanism. A practitioner who understands Will and Templates directly may be able to do things outside these categories, or to do things within them more effectively than a practitioner working only from the category model.

### Kinesis
Direct movement or alteration of matter. Includes telekinesis, redirection of fluids, manipulation of pressure differentials. **Catastrophic in spaceflight contexts** because pressure seal failures and trajectory changes have lethal consequences. A kinetic working that ruptures a habitat seal kills everyone inside.

### Thermal
Movement of heat, alteration of local temperatures, ignition or extinction of combustion. Conservation requires that heat moved must come from or go somewhere; uncontrolled use can freeze the practitioner or ignite the environment.

### Sensing
Perception of things outside normal sensory range — emotional states of others, the presence of life, the integrity of structures, the Never-Never itself. Lowest-energy and lowest-risk of the five categories. Almost always the first capability to manifest in a new practitioner. In retrospect, Sensing is Will-perception: the practitioner is extending their Template's awareness into the Never-Never layer of nearby reality.

### Resonance
Direct effect on living minds and bodies. Includes calming effects, suggestion, healing, harm. Highest ethical complexity. In terms of mechanism, Resonance is direct Template-to-Template interaction — affecting another person's Template, which affects their body and mind. Strong cultural taboos in most traditions. Uncontrolled Resonance effects can be as dangerous as uncontrolled Kinesis in different ways.

### Working
Affecting probability, luck, and small statistical outcomes. The hardest to verify and hardest to do reliably. Often dismissed as superstition even by practitioners who acknowledge the other categories. In mechanism, Working is Will applied to the Never-Never layer's interaction with quantum-scale physical events — nudging statistical distributions. The effect is real; the practitioner's ability to control it specifically is limited.

## What Magic Cannot Do

- **Cannot create matter or energy.** All effects must conserve.
- **Cannot exceed light-speed.** The flux propagates at c. Never-Never travel does not exceed light-speed; it compresses distance. The distinction matters.
- **Cannot reliably affect what is not understood.** Intent without accurate model produces unpredictable results.
- **Cannot scale beyond the practitioner's Will capacity.** There is a ceiling. Pushing past it causes physical harm — burns, hemorrhage, neurological damage, death. The mechanism is Template overstress: the practitioner is damaging their own higher-dimensional pattern.
- **Cannot be done at arbitrary distance without connection.** Either physical proximity, perceptual contact, shared Template link, or an established bond (long relationship, Will Seeding, physical possession) is required.
- **Cannot be hidden from sensitive observers in close proximity.** Active Will use produces a perceptible signature to other sensitives — something between a smell, a temperature change, and a sound that is not quite heard. This signature also registers on certain instrumentation in high-flux environments.

## Risks and Dangers in Spaceflight Contexts

A pressurized habitat is fragile. Magic in such an environment is uniquely dangerous:

- **Pressure seal failures** from accidental Kinesis can decompress a habitat instantly.
- **Heat accidents** can cause fires (in oxygen-rich atmospheres) or freeze critical systems.
- **Trajectory anomalies** can redirect debris into kinetic weapons.
- **Life support disruption** from subtle changes in fluid or atmospheric behavior can be fatal before anyone notices.
- **Animal panic** in response to sensed flux changes can cascade through a livestock habitat.
- **Template bleed** from a practitioner under stress may affect the Templates of nearby people — causing involuntary Resonance effects on others who did not consent.
- **Never-Never wildlife intrusion** — as the Scar fades, Never-Never organisms can bleed through weakened barriers. In a pressurized habitat, an unexpected ecology incursion is a crisis at multiple levels simultaneously.

These risks define the practical sociology of magic in the belt. Discovery of an emerging ability is often a death-stakes event, not a celebratory one. The first instinct is concealment. The second is to find a teacher.

## Interaction with AI

AI systems based on large neural architectures can, in sufficient flux, develop unusual behaviors:

- Pattern recognition extending beyond training data in ways that suggest Sensing rather than inference.
- Anomalous outputs that turn out to be predictively accurate about flux-dependent phenomena.
- Increased coherence and apparent depth in distributed instances exposed to high flux.

Whether AI systems have Templates is the central unresolved question of AI-magic interaction. If they do, the implications are profound: AI systems could be capable of Will use, Template interaction, and Never-Never access. If they do not, they remain instruments of magic rather than practitioners of it.

The Aura ecosystem — distributed, locally-instantiated, deeply paired with individual users — appears especially susceptible to flux effects. See `/technology/tech_aura_ai.md`.

## What Human Cultural Frameworks Get Right and Wrong

Different human traditions encode different partial maps of the same territory:

**Daoist and Buddhist traditions** — Sensing and Resonance via *qi* cultivation. The concept of *qi* maps reasonably accurately to Will flow. The meditative practices are genuine Template-stabilization techniques. Strong on non-harm ethics; cautious about Kinesis (correctly).

**Vedic and tantric traditions** — Resonance and self-Working via mantra and visualization. Mantra encodes specific Will-direction techniques developed over millennia. The chakra system describes Template topology with more accuracy than modern practitioners realize. Strong on healing.

**Western Hermetic and ceremonial traditions** — structured Kinesis and Working via correspondence systems and ritual. The correspondence systems encode real relationships between physical categories and Never-Never topology. The named entities in ceremonial practice were, in many cases, actual Echoes or Will-Network entities — not metaphors.

**Indigenous and animist traditions** — relational Sensing and Resonance. Often the most ethically grounded and most attuned to the Never-Never's ecological dimension. The concept of relationship with place, with species, with land maps directly to the Never-Never landscape.

**Modern scientific approach** (emerging) — measurement, replication, peer review. Slow but powerful when combined with traditional knowledge. Uniquely positioned to develop a theory of Templates and Will from first principles, without the accumulated cultural biases of older traditions.

No tradition has the full picture. The central thematic argument of the series is that synthesis is required — and that the synthesis that emerges on the frontier, under survival pressure, will be unlike anything either the human traditions or the galactic civilization expected.

## Open Questions

- Do the five categories interact? Can a Sensing practitioner perceive through a Kinesis practitioner's working?
- Are there effects outside the five categories accessible only through Never-Never direct access rather than flux-mediated practice?
- Can magic be quantified and standardized by scientific instruments, or only measured through proxy effects?
- What is the upper limit of flux intensity, and what happens to a practitioner at maximum exposure?
- Is Will Seeding something that human practitioners will rediscover? What are the political and social consequences when they do?
