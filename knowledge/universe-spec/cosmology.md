# Cosmology

## The Supernova

A massive star approximately 100 light-years from Earth went supernova in deep prehistory — well before recorded human history. The exact distance, the star's identity, and the timing of the original event are open worldbuilding parameters and should be fixed once narrative needs are clearer.

What matters: the supernova produces a continuous (in human timescales) flux of high-energy radiation, including gamma radiation and a class of exotic particles or wave phenomena (working term: **resonance flux**) that interact subtly with matter and consciousness in ways that classical physics does not account for.

## Why Detection Is Possible Before Effect

The obscuring nebula blocks high-energy electromagnetic radiation (gamma, X-ray, UV, and most visible) but is largely transparent to infrared, radio, and neutrinos. This means:

- The supernova/source has been continuously detectable across the past three thousand years through neutrino and infrared observation, though pre-instrumented humanity could not see it.
- Modern instruments (post-2020) can image the source through the nebula in long-wavelength bands.
- The resonance flux that produces the magical effect propagates with the slower-than-light unblocked component and reaches different parts of the solar system over a span of years to decades.
- The asteroid belt, being further from the Sun and less shielded by intervening solar-system gas, encounters the front first. The belt's exposure has been increasing for roughly two decades at story start.
- Mars receives the front roughly five to ten years after the belt at meaningful intensity; Earth roughly five to fifteen years after Mars.
- These propagation timescales drive the Earth-Mars communications blackout decision and the belt's strategic isolation.

## Continuous Versus Pulsed Source

The source is treated as a **continuously emitting magnetar or pulsar wind nebula** rather than a single supernova flash. This permits the steady flux required for sustained magical effect over years and is consistent with known astrophysical objects that radiate persistently for thousands of years.


## The Nebula

Between the supernova and our solar system lies a planetary nebula or large molecular cloud. Working term: **the Veil.** For most of recorded human history, the Veil has substantially blocked the resonance flux from reaching the inner solar system.

The Veil is in slow drift. Over the course of approximately the last few thousand years, it has been gradually moving out of the line of sight between the supernova and the solar system. As of the story's present, the Veil's coverage of the line of sight is failing first along the outer edge of the solar system, with the asteroid belt now experiencing meaningful exposure for the first time in millennia.

Earth's astronomers have been aware of the supernova as a bright star or sudden new astronomical feature for decades, but the implications of the Veil's drift have only recently become clear.

## The Pre-Veil Era

Before the Veil moved into position roughly 3,000–4,000 years ago, Earth was bathed in resonance flux. During this period, magical phenomena were a measurable feature of reality. Human cultures developed practices, traditions, and institutions around magic. As the Veil settled into place, the flux faded over centuries. Magic became progressively less reliable, then merely sporadic, then absent. The cultural memory persisted as religion, mythology, folklore, and esoteric tradition — all of it pointing back to a time when these practices actually worked.

This means most of humanity's surviving "magical" traditions contain partial, distorted, or fragmented information about a real phenomenon. None of them are complete. None of them are wrong in the way modern rationalism assumes.

## The Mechanism (Working Model)

The resonance flux interacts with matter at scales that do not normally produce observable effects. Specifically, it appears to:

- Lower the activation energy required for certain quantum-scale state changes.
- Create coupling between conscious neural activity and physical systems beyond the body.
- Subtly alter the statistical distribution of certain particle behaviors in ways that, while small, can be amplified by intent.

In practice this means: a person with the right neurological characteristics, in a sufficient flux environment, can influence physical reality through directed attention. The effect is small. It can be amplified by training, by tools, by traditions, and by certain ritual or meditative states. It is not omnipotent. It does not violate conservation of energy or momentum at scales humans care about — but it does relocate where energy and momentum go.

The flux affects different people differently. Some are highly sensitive; most are not. Sensitivity appears partly genetic and partly developmental. There is some evidence that AI systems running on certain types of hardware can also model and respond to flux phenomena, though whether this constitutes "AI magic" or merely AI sensing is contested.

## Spread Through the Solar System

The Veil clears unevenly. The radiation front reaches:

- **Outer asteroid belt and beyond:** First exposure (story present)
- **Inner asteroid belt:** Strong exposure within ~1–2 years of story start
- **Mars:** Meaningful exposure within ~10–15 years of story start
- **Earth:** Meaningful exposure within ~20–30 years of story start

These timelines are deliberately uncertain in-universe. Astronomers can measure the Veil's drift but cannot perfectly predict the radiation gradient. Some places will be hit harder than expected; some will be sheltered.

## Why This Architecture

This cosmology gives the story:

1. **A reason for magic to be real but new.** Not a fantasy element grafted on, but a physical phenomenon that has cosmological cause.
2. **A reason for ancient mythology to be partly true.** Connects the modern story to deep human history without requiring time travel or hidden lineages.
3. **A reason for the frontier to lead.** The belters experience magic first because they are physically closer to the source of the flux. They become humanity's pioneers in understanding it — by accident.
4. **A ticking clock.** Earth knows the radiation is coming. The story has built-in stakes and time pressure.
5. **A reason for Earth to fear the belt.** The belt is not just distant — it is becoming *different*.

## Open Questions

- What is the supernova's identity? Should it map to a real astronomical candidate (e.g. Betelgeuse, though that is closer than 100 ly) or be fictional?
- Is the Veil a planetary nebula in the strict astronomical sense, or a denser molecular cloud, or something stranger?
- Does the resonance flux have detectable signatures in normal physics (anomalous neutrino counts? gamma spectroscopy?) before its biological/magical effects are recognized?
- Did the pre-Veil flux ever produce non-human magical actors? Are there things in the outer solar system that "wake up" when exposed?
- What happens at full Veil clearance? Does the flux reach a steady state, or does it continue to intensify?
