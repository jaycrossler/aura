#### S-02 — After

**Summary:** Jin comes through after the mop-up is confirmed. He looks at Sophia.
He says something in Mandarin. His {Aura} translates, with a brief lag: *"You
killed a very large spider."* Sophia says: *"Yes."* Jin says: *"Good."* This is
the beginning of a friendship.

Later, talking to Jace while they wait for medical updates, Jin mentions the
three months of nest-clearing. Eleven nests. Drill lances. His crew's hands.
Jace's reaction is the reader's reaction.

**Must accomplish:**
1. Aftermath is specific — injuries physical and otherwise; no one is fine
2. Jin/Sophia exchange is the emotional pivot of the aftermath
3. Nick's aftermath is shown without drama — he crossed against every instinct
   and then sat on the floor; this is honorable
4. The nest-clearing revelation happens here, in conversation, as context

#### S-02b — The Planning Session / Kael Revealed

**Summary:** Nick's {Aura} module produces the alert — three minutes of tactical
warning, plus a longer build-up window giving approximately two hours for
planning. The planning session is the first time Jace, Sophia, Jin, and Nick are
in the same room, and the first time Jace and Sophia tell the station's defenders
what they have been doing.

The chaos is real. Sophia pushes an object across a table without touching it.
Jin's crew does not look surprised — they look like people for whom a
long-running mystery has just been named. Li Hao says something in Mandarin. Shi
Gang says nothing.

During the session: Jace mentions, a few times, the woman standing near him. He
mentions her conversationally, as you mention someone in a crowded room. *"The
woman by the door might have a better vantage point."* He stops. No one is
looking where he indicated. He mentions it two more times. Three total. Each
time, the room responds to everything he says except the reference to her.

{Penny} has been sending Jace unauthorized-intrusion alerts. He has been
silencing them. Nine in forty minutes. The entry points are moving.

Sophia notices Jace's expression during the third alert he silences. She crosses
into the Astral.

**What Sophia sees:** The woman is there. In the Astral she is brilliant — not
warm-bright, brilliant the way that is several orders of magnitude removed from
everyone else in the room. Sophia has never seen a Will-signature like this. She
is reaching into Jace's Template. Sophia sees the egg — sees it for the first
time, clear and horrible, lodged there since X-02a — and sees the woman remove
it. Clean, immediate, complete. In physical space, Jace blinks. He says something
to {Aura} that doesn't connect to anything anyone said. He looks like someone who
has come in from somewhere very cold. Then he shakes his head and pays attention.

AH Level drops from 4.5 to 0. Jace does not know what changed.

**Must accomplish:**
- Kael is in the planning session before anyone knows she is there
- Sophia tears the occultation ("Mind Web") with an improvised force pull — her
  plan to continue observing undetected ends there
- Kael's absence of alarm is what makes her frightening, not her capability
- Kael cannot understand English; no one can understand her; zero vocabulary at
  Arc 1 close — the translation project is Arc 2 material

> and reveal, and it directly conflicts with the "Many undecided chapters" scratch
> notes below (items 6 and 7). Both cannot be true. See Open Decision #8a in the
> master file. This restructure does NOT choose between them.**
