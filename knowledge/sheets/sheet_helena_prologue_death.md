# Helena — Prologue Death (Vault Snapshot)

## Story Context

Helena, an untrained latent sensitive, undergoes an accidental sleep-adjacent
crossing during the transcendent peak of her daily yoga class. Under the
duress and disorientation of her first uncontrolled crossing, she unknowingly
triggers a Self-Template store — an accidental vault copy, with no training,
intent, or awareness that anything has happened. She then loses her bearings
in the Astral and steps outside the station's Astral-equivalent footprint,
where she dies of exposure. No physical breach occurs; she was never in
physical space at the moment of death, preserving the locked-room mystery
investigated by Suzi (see [[char_suzi]]).

This sheet is the vault baseline for her eventual resurrection (see
[[event_helena_epilogue]] and [[sheet_helena_epilogue_resurrection]]).

## What This Vault Captures

- Full memory and personality up through the end of yoga class and the
  beginning of her walk to the bar to meet friends for Sophia's birthday party
- No training, no conscious magical skill, no [Self Template] proficiency —
  the store is a raw, unrefined accident, not a deliberate practitioner's
  vault
- Physical condition: healthy, mid-20s, no injuries

## What This Vault Does NOT Capture

- Anything after she began heading toward the bar — the party itself, her
  final hours, and her death are not part of the stored pattern
- Any skill, memory, or relationship development that would have occurred
  after this moment
