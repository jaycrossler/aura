# Jace Grant — Arc 1 Start (Falcon Departure)

## Story Context

Jace Apollo Grant, 26, has just been medically discharged from the USAF
under circumstances that destroyed his intended career. He boards the *Falcon*
at Mars with a five-year Origin Industries contract, a military-grade HUD
implant he is still paying off, his dog Cerberus, and a half-built survey
drone. He has no awareness of the Astral and no deliberate magic ability.
His Will has been generating for 26 years with nowhere to go. He does not
know this. He is heading to the belt because there is nowhere else to go.
