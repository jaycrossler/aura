# Helena — Epilogue Resurrection

## Story Context

Helena is restored from the accidental vault snapshot taken during her
Prologue yoga class (see [[sheet_helena_prologue_death]]). Per the standard
resurrection constraints in [[magic_will_and_templates]], she retains nothing
after the vault's storage moment and her immediate post-resurrection memories
are blurry. She has no idea a resurrection has occurred and instead
interprets her state as an unusually severe hangover.

## What She Retains

- Full memory and personality up through the end of yoga class and the start
  of her walk to the bar — matches the vault baseline exactly
- All pre-existing relationships, skills, and knowledge of the station

## What She Has Lost

- Sophia's birthday party in full — she has no memory of attending
- Any events, conversations, or time that passed on Fortuna during her death
  and the intervening period (duration TBD — see Open Notes)
- Any knowledge of her own death or the mystery investigation Suzi has been
  running

## Post-Resurrection State

- Physical: cold, headache, disoriented — misattributed by Helena to a
  hangover
- Blurry immediate memory consistent with the standard resurrection
  constraint; she does not question the gap deeply, filling it with the
  hangover explanation rather than alarm
- No awareness of [Self Template] mechanics, her own accidental store, or
  the fact that she is a resurrection case — this is a reveal-later thread

## Progression Axis Summary

| Axis | Level | Notes |

| Toolchain | none | Unchanged |
| Social | established | Unchanged, though the resurrection itself will likely alter how the station relates to her going forward |
| Environmental | established | Unchanged |
