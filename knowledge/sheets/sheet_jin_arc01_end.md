# Jin Lóng — Arc 1 End (Kael Arrival)

## Story Context

Jin Lóng has been the head mining supervisor of Fortuna's Chinese detachment
for years, carrying the weight of {Seek}'s surveillance and coercive control
while trying to protect his crew. The station feared his miners — saw armed,
isolated people acting strangely and filled in the gaps with suspicion. Jin
knew this and could not explain without making it worse.

During the swarm attack, everything changed. {Seek}'s local infrastructure
was destroyed by Astral predators during the fight. The moment it went offline,
Jin wept. His crew wept. And then they fought. His thermal manipulation
abilities — logged by {Seek} for eight months, unfiled by Beijing, unknown to
Jin himself as anything other than something he suppressed — emerged at full
intensity in the chaos. Fire in the Astral. Fire in the corridors.

He does not fully understand what happened to him. He understands that he is
free. He understands that the station he has been defending for years is still
standing. That is enough for now.

## Resources & Constraints

- **Status:** Chinese state mining contract. Now with {Seek} destroyed, his
  contractual situation is complex. Beijing's apparatus has been severed from
  Fortuna's network. He has not received instructions. He is, for the first
  time, operating without oversight. He doesn't know if this is freedom or
  abandonment.
- **Equipment:** Standard mining supervisor kit; suit; tools. His crew has
  the mining equipment. No Astral-specific equipment — everything he did, he
  did bare-handed.
- **The Big Strike:** The gold vein discovery four years ago is what kept
  China invested in Fortuna. Jin found it. The windfall is his legacy here,
  for good and ill.
