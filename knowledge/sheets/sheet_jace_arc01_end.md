# Jace Grant — Arc 1 End (Kael Arrival)

## Story Context

The swarm has been repelled. The Astral Scavenging Queen — a cryptid of
horrifying scale that was pushing the swarm through the edge of the Scar —
has been killed. Jace fought it with the tools he built: an aluminum-frame
rocket buggy, improvised hydrogen-fed thrusters, the multi-camera rig, and
Cerberus. Sophia killed the Queen's orb (or held the line while Jace did —
TBD choreography). Nick's {Aura} modifications held the station's AI network
together during the fight.

Jace is alive. He has burns, a documented Astral injury from the Tick-Maw
fight, and the specific exhaustion of someone who has spent months building
toward a crisis and then survived it. Kael has just arrived, tailing the
swarm and expecting to find ruins. She found something else.
