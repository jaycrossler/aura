# Prologue — Helena

## Scene Function

Framing device. Helena is the reader's first guide to Fortuna Station — an
ordinary, beloved local living an ordinary day — before the story shifts to
Jace's POV in Chapter 1. Through her routine, the reader absorbs orientation
information without exposition: this is a space station, it sits above an
asteroid, there are animals aboard, pressure seals require constant informal
vigilance, and a ship called the *Falcon* is inbound and expected to dock soon.

The scene ends by stating plainly that Helena is dead. No death is shown. No
body, no breach, no explanation — consistent with the locked-room mystery
established in [[char_helena]] and investigated by [[char_suzi]].

## Must Accomplish (Arc 1 Prequel Contract)

1. Establish Helena's backstory: first child born on Fortuna Station, grew up
   there, knows everyone; her parents left her their earnings and others have
   gifted money to her, making her the richest person on the station
2. Show her daily yoga class in the park as a beloved fixture of station life
3. Show her wandering the station on informal jobs — checking pressure seals,
   feeding animals, helping with small tasks — establishing her as woven into
   the community's daily functioning
4. Orient the reader: they are on a space station, positioned above an
   asteroid; animals live aboard; the *Falcon* is inbound and due to dock soon
5. Establish her excitement for tonight's party — celebrating Sophia's 30th
   birthday — and her plan to flirt with Jin
6. During yoga class, show her chakras aligning and a moment of transcendence
   — described as a beautiful, positive experience, with no indication to the
   reader (or to Helena) that anything is wrong
7. She heads to the bar, still happy, to meet her friends
8. State plainly, without a death scene: she is dead

## Beats

### Beat 1 — Morning Rounds
Helena moves through the station doing the small, informal jobs that make her
feel useful and connect her to everyone: checking a pressure seal reading here,
tossing feed to an animal there, trading gossip and small favors. This is the
vehicle for orienting the reader to the physical world — the rotating ring, the
hum of the station, the fact that they are anchored above an asteroid, animals
underfoot, the *Falcon*'s docking window coming up soon. Everyone knows her.
She is generous, grounded, and completely at home here in a way no one else on
the station — soon to include Jace — will be.

### Beat 2 — Yoga Class
Her daily low-g yoga class in the park. This is the heart of her identity and
her contribution to the community. Near the end of class, in a held pose, she
feels something extraordinary — her chakras align, and she is briefly
transcendent. It reads as a spiritual high point of her day, not a warning
sign. (This moment is the unwitting trigger for an accidental Self-Template
store — see [[magic_will_and_templates]] addendum — but this must not be
signaled to the reader here.)

### Beat 3 — Anticipation
Still glowing from class, she thinks ahead to tonight: Sophia's 30th birthday
party. She plans to finally make a real move on flirting with Jin — the
stoic, heavily-muscled miner she's had her eye on. She's looking forward to
teasing him properly tonight instead of just orbiting him at the edges the way
she usually does.

### Beat 4 — The Bar
She arrives at the bar, happy, meets her friends, the party is starting. This
is the last we see of her, alive.

### Beat 5 — The Statement
A clean break from her POV. The reader is told, plainly and without spectacle,
that Helena is dead. No mechanism, no scene, no discovery. The tone should
land as genuine loss, not mystery-box intrigue — grief first, questions later.

## Must NOT Do

- Must not depict, imply, or foreshadow a death scene
- Must not show or reference a body, a breach, an EVA, or a mechanism of death
- Must not use `[Skill]` bracket notation — this is long before Jace
  establishes the convention in-story
- Must not mention Vince or any named romantic interest beyond Jin
- Must not tip the reader off that the transcendence moment during yoga is
  significant beyond being a nice moment in her day
