# Session Notes — 2026-05-02 (Voice Session, Rich + Early Scenes + Cerberus + Hard Months)

A single voice session covering Rich's expansion, early-arrival scene texture, Cerberus integration, gig-work specifics, and the motivational arc that drives Jace toward the Origin contract. Conducted in three loose rounds; all changes are now staged.

---

## Resolved (All Rounds)

### Character Canon
- **Rich's kid count:** Canonized at THREE children (was inconsistent — bio file said two, voice file said three). Bio file patched.
- **Rich's role at the base:** Lead officer for base IT and communications on the joint U.S. Air Force / Space Force installation. Not just a comms specialist.
- **Rich's compassion trait:** Now explicit in character file. Smiles often, baseline warmth, acts on his concern within bounded judgment.
- **Rich's coffee canister:** Iconic mannerism. Large insulated canister carried everywhere, drunk frequently. Signals work-ethic and mild insomnia.
- **Cerberus's gender:** Male. (Resolved conflict with deprecated `char_dog_alpha.md` which had her as female.)
- **Cerberus's breed lineage:** Herding dog × pug cross.
- **Cerberus's physical traits:** Short hair, bred not to bark, very expressive wrinkly face, big nostrils, loves to run, cuddles up against Jace when sleeping.
- **Cerberus's pre-negotiation bond:** Jace has weeks of attachment at the dog breeder facility BEFORE the Origin negotiation. The puppy is named on a walk home from one of those visits.
- **The Rat:** Canonical primary handle for the smuggler character. "Heart of gold, pocket of more gold" framing added.

### Location Canon
- **Pangu / Pengu naming:** Pangu is the official Chinese settlement name (already canonical). Pengu is the colloquial nickname used widely, including by the Chinese themselves.
- **Pangu reputation:** Industrially polluted; oily clothes, fuel-contaminated atmosphere, refinery smell. Workers recognizable across other Mars settlements by stained coveralls. Free government-provided food for all residents and visitors.
- **Joint U.S. Air Force / Space Force base at Terminus:** Formalized. Multiple connected and stand-alone domes, extensive tunnel network, 60–70 year history, drone operations + robot control + satellite + orbital traffic functions.
- **Service rivalry:** Air Force (blue uniforms) vs. Space Force (black-and-silver) — banter at working level, friction at command level. Colonel (Space Force) commands; Captain (Air Force, TBD) supervises Rich and Jace directly.
- **Coffee Tree Dome (Terminus):** Specialty atmospheric conditions, Mars coffee cultivation, Rich's favorite stop on tours.
- **Bird Dome (Terminus):** Higher atmospheric pressure for bird flight, taller dome, fake lake, geese with goslings, 100-credit entry per person, home of the Mars Goose Cam streaming phenomenon.
- **Mars Dog Breeder Facility (Terminus):** Site of the Mars dog breeding program (~30 years of selection, herding × pug). Where Jace meets Cerberus's family during the hard months.

### World Canon
- **Mars economy & manufacturing:** Repair-and-reuse culture. 3D printing works for structural/mechanical/simple things; does NOT work for complex pharmaceuticals, specialized foods, or advanced biologics. Recycling feeds back into printer feedstock. Labs and chemical factories handle the irreplaceable stuff.
- **Mars corporate presence:** Almost a thousand American drone aircraft on Mars total; military flies roughly half, corporations fly the rest. Origin Industries, Stellar Dynamics, and smaller players have leased pads at the launch facility, fab district, and elsewhere.
- **Military address culture:** "LT" is a compassionate nickname for lieutenants used by junior enlisted and (sometimes) superiors as a sign of warmth. Lieutenants do NOT call peers "LT" — they use first names. Full convention canonized in [Military Address Culture — USAF and Space Force on Mars](../universe-spec/concept_military_address_culture.md).

### Scene & Plot Canon
- **Family-messaging beat merged into the gym scene.** Jace multitasks during the treadmill run: HUD game + family video messages (Dawn, Chris, Athena, including asks about Jelly and Jinx) + repeatedly failed Artemis sends. Oblivious to the two girls trying to flirt. `event_jace_messages_home.md` deprecated.
- **Drone center tour exists as its own scene** (`event_jace_drone_center_tour.md`) — six operators, fleet scale, Mei/Sylvester glimpse, "almost a thousand drones / half corporate" line.
- **Rich's comms favor exists as its own scene** (`event_rich_comms_favor.md`) — post-discharge, off-base, Rich uses his IT/comms access to do Jace one significant favor. Specific favor mechanism flagged for author selection.
- **Dog breeder visits exist as a recurring scene** (`event_jace_dog_breeder_visits.md`) — runs across the hard months, foreshadows Cerberus, establishes Jace as a dog person.
- **Horse farm offer exists as its own scene** (`event_jace_horse_farm_offer.md`) — parents offer to sell, Jace refuses, refusal drives the Origin contract decision. The motivational hinge of Jace's path to the belt.
- **Jace's gig work specifics:** Algae vat cleaning, wiring repair, restaurant back-of-house, one-time Pangu trip. Each gig adds texture. Pangu trip notable for the free Chinese government-provided food.
- **Grant family on Earth:** Horse farm in Virginia. Family dog is **Jelly** (older mixed-breed, Athena named her). Family horse is **Jinx** (Chris's gelding, the horse Jace and his sisters learned to ride on). Both anchored to the farm; both load-bearing for the horse farm offer beat.
