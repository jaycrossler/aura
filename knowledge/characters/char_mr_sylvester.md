# Mr. Sylvester

## Summary

Mr. Sylvester is the American intelligence handler who recruits Jace Grant as an asset and runs Mei's belt operation from Mars. He is sympathetic to Jace's situation in the way that a skilled recruiter is sympathetic — genuinely, but functionally. He will use Jace if useful. He believes the mission matters. He is not entirely wrong about this.

He is the kind of man who knows exactly what leverage he has over people and applies it precisely. He knew Jace's financial situation, medical condition, and psychological profile before their first meeting. The offer he made was calibrated. He is not cruel about this; he simply operates in a world where information and leverage are the primary currencies.

## What He Knows

At the time he recruits Jace, Sylvester has:
- Full access to Jace's military service record, medical file, and financial situation.
- Knowledge of the Artemis connection — Jace's sister at Odysseus Station may be relevant to the operation independently.
- A working assessment of Jace's personality: reliable under structure, directional, not naturally deceptive but capable of following instructions.
- An understanding that Jace's reactivated HUD is a significant inducement and a significant lever.

What he may not know: whether Jace will eventually choose his own judgment over Sylvester's instructions, and how Mei's growing doubt about the mission framing will interact with Jace's inevitable learning curve.

## The Mission Framing

Sylvester presents the belt posting to both Mei and Jace as: investigate Chinese sabotage, develop sources, report back. He believes this framing is accurate. He may be wrong. He may also have information he has not shared that would change both of their assessments.

Whether Sylvester is acting entirely in good faith or has a secondary agenda — related to the anomalies, to the returning magic, to institutional positioning — is deliberately open.

## Voice and Mannerisms

- **Speech:** Measured. Speaks at a pace that suggests he chose each word. No verbal tics.
- **Demeanor:** Warm in a contained way. Listens more than he speaks. When he speaks, it lands.
- **With assets:** Treats them as capable adults while retaining all the structural leverage. This is more effective than most people expect.

## Open Questions

- His full name and organizational home within U.S. intelligence.
- Whether he has prior history with Artemis Grant, or whether that connection is new to him.
- Whether he privately believes the anomalies are something other than sabotage, and has chosen not to say so.
- His eventual response when the magical reality becomes undeniable.
