# Athena Grant

## Summary

Athena is the youngest Grant sibling, still at university on Earth when the story begins. She appears in the family video call that bookmarks Jace's Mars arrival — congratulating him, proud, a little envious of his adventure without yet understanding its cost. She is connected to Jace through family warmth and an increasingly infrequent communication chain.

Her role in early books is small — a voice on a call, a detail of home. Her significance will grow as the flux front reaches Earth in the later story arc. She is the Grant family's representative in the world that will be transformed last.
