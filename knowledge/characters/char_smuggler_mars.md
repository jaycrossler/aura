# The Smuggler (Mars Launch Facility)

## Summary

The smuggler is a launch facility fixture — one of those people who knows every corner of the Mars orbital infrastructure, has worked for three different employers in twenty years, and exists in the productive grey space between official operations and informal ones. Artemis Grant has used this person's services before and trusts them with a specific, bounded trust: they are reliable within their code, which is practical and self-interested rather than principled.

When Jace delivers Artemis's package, the smuggler takes it without comment, pays Jace, and files him away as potentially useful. When the emergency belt dropout opens, the smuggler calls in a contact, gets Jace the slot, and runs a compressed animal handling certification that is legally sufficient if not examined too closely.

The smuggler's motivation: Artemis's gratitude, Jace's demonstrated ability to follow instructions without asking too many questions, and the small financial consideration that comes with filling an emergency slot. This is not altruism. It is the informal economy operating as designed.

## The Certification

The animal handling crash course is real instruction compressed to weeks. The smuggler has genuine knowledge — possibly from prior ferry work or time on a Mars agricultural station. Jace learns enough to be functional. The documentation is complete. No one at Origin has reason to look closely, especially with an emergency vacancy to fill.
