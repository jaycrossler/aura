# Dr. Lanchee

## Summary

Lanchee is the *Falcon*'s ship's doctor. She is short, precise, and almost never in the common areas. Her days are spent on medical consultations with Fortuna Station — reviewing patient files, advising the station's medics, building the professional relationships she'll need when she arrives. She is already doing her job. She is just doing it from 200 million kilometers away.

Siren has adopted her cabin as a secondary residence. Lanchee has not objected.

She is one of four humans on a ship with fifty-eight women, and she is the one Jace knows least. He is fairly sure she is aware of everything that happens on the ship anyway. Doctors usually are.
