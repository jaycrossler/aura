# Dr. Lanchee

## Summary

Lanchee is the *Falcon*'s ship's doctor. She is short, precise, and almost never in the
common areas. Her days are spent on medical consultations with Fortuna Station —
reviewing patient files, advising the station's medics, building the professional
relationships she'll need when she arrives. She is already doing her job. She is just
doing it from 200 million kilometers away.

Her home, clinic, and supplies all live in one canister module. It is, functionally, a
clinic that happens to also be where she sleeps — and it will move with her, unchanged,
when she eventually transfers to Fortuna Station.

Siren has adopted her canister as a secondary residence. Lanchee has not objected.

She is one of four humans on a ship with fifty-eight women, and she is the one Jace
knows least. He is fairly sure she is aware of everything that happens on the ship
anyway. Doctors usually are.
