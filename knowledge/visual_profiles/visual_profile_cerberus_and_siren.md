## Breed and Physical Basis

**Belgian Malinois × Pug hybrid** — lightly genetically engineered for low-gravity
habitats over a ~30-year Mars breeding programme.

The cross produces:
- The Malinois's athletic build, lighter frame, and longer snout
- The Pug's wrinkly face, compact body, and curly tail
- Modified: extended snout for better breathing (the Pug flat-face is gone);
  short non-shedding coat (engineered out for life-support reasons); large nostrils
- The result: short, squat, muscular, tough dogs with wrinkled faces, athletic Malinois
  bodies underneath, and pig-like curly tails that are slightly ridiculous

### What Cerberus Looks Like
A compact, muscular dog with a wrinkled face that's somehow both pug and athletic.
The extended snout saves him from the pug breathing issues but keeps the wrinkles
and the expressive face. Darker fawn brindle — the black overlay is heavy enough
that in dim lighting he reads as a dark dog with tan markings rather than a tan dog
with black markings. Big puppy paws at Arc 1 start that he's still growing into.
The curly tail is the thing that makes people smile first.

### Cerberus Behaviour
- **Bonds exclusively with Jace**; waits for Jace's nod before sniffing strangers
- Always wants a paw touching Jace's leg or shoulder; climbs onto the couch back
  to put his chin on Jace's shoulder
- Sleeps on Jace's bed regardless of the existence of the dog bed (which Jace bought and
  Cerberus has never used)
- **Rarely barks** — bred quiet; the mess-hall bark that startles Sophia is one of
  the first times Jace has heard it; when he does bark it is surprisingly loud and
  sharp, with a growl following
- Cocks his head when puzzled or interested; the head tilt is his primary "I am
  processing this" expression
- **Fascinated by pig tails** — the Barn is a special place for him partly because
  of this
- Silly and clownish in play; reserved and watchful in new situations

### Cerberus in Low Gravity (1/3g)
Bounces higher than intended; crashes into walls and tables; launches himself at
objects and overshoots; gradually adapts over the Falcon transit but never stops
having the occasional spectacular miscalculation. Jace shakes his head and cleans
things up. This becomes a recurring bit.

### The Cerberus-Jace Daily Loop (Falcon)
Jace runs the ring in his weighted compression suit. Cerberus runs his own circuits —
a small brindle dog doing independent laps while one tall blond human jogs the same
corridor. After the run: both collapse. Cerberus falls asleep in the cafeteria where
Jace made two fluffy dog beds. Cerberus uses the bed, then migrates to Jace's feet.

  wants to sniff everyone; immediately approaches strangers

  and greeting barks (contrast with Cerberus's near-silence)
- **Sniffy and smiley** — always nose-down investigating, tail going
- Shows up unexpectedly anywhere on the station; sometimes with Mei, sometimes at
  the Inn, sometimes in a random resident's lap, often by the animal pens
- **The Barn:** Claims the Barn entry as her domain; accompanies every child who
  enters; barks once (clearly, not aggressively) if a child feeds an animal
  incorrectly; station children have learned to listen
- **Queen of the neighbourhood** — everyone loves her; she knows it and is magnanimous about it

## Shared Behaviours

### The Nap-Run-Nap Cycle
Nap → run for an hour → nap → repeat. In 1/3g they occasionally launch higher than
intended, bounce off walls, and crash into tables. Jace shakes his head. They don't
stop. Post-run they fall asleep in the cafeteria on the fluffy dog beds Jace made for them.

### The Tail
When excited: vibrates at the base; sometimes uncurls slightly and wags. When alert
or spooked: stays curled but the back hair rises along the spine. Looks slightly
ridiculous at all times in an endearing way. Cerberus is particularly fascinated by
actual pig tails when he sees them for comparison.

### Head Tilt
Both dogs cock their heads to one side when interested or trying to understand
something. Cerberus does it with more gravitas. Siren does it more often.

### Food
Both infinitely food-motivated. Both get a bit pudgy over time. Both will vibrate
when food is near.

## The Kai-Cerberus First Meeting

In physical space, the meeting looks like two animals about to fight:
- Posturing, rotating around each other, both baring teeth
- Cerberus growls and his back hair rises; tail points straight up
- Kai pulls back lips to show very sharp teeth and blows breath at Cerberus
- They look like they are permanently thirty seconds from an attack

Approximately 30 minutes later, they are curled up together. Cerberus is on his back,
snoring, somehow curled up in Kai's tail.

This is how they are from then on. The posturing phase never entirely goes away but
it shortens. It is social ritual, not actual hostility.
