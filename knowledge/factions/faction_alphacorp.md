# AlphaCorp

## Summary

AlphaCorp is what Alphabet became: the solar system's information layer. Where
Origin Industries owns *things moving* — cargo, fabrication, fulfillment —
AlphaCorp owns *knowing*: search, maps, navigation solutions, comms indexing,
advertising, and the sensor-fusion products that every station and ship quietly
depends on. Its consumer face is {Gem}.

## {Gem}

AlphaCorp's AI assistant. Polished, helpful, omnipresent in AlphaCorp-serviced
zones and mandatory on AlphaCorp charter contracts — which is why Maureen, who
hates carrying any AI, has to load {Gem} when she takes AlphaCorp runs, the same
way Chinese contracts force {Seek} aboard. Contractors who work for multiple
conglomerates end up carrying a rotating cast of AIs, none of which share data
with each other — a seam people learn to exploit (see [[concept_ai_seams]]).

## Relationship to Origin

Cordial in press releases, adversarial in telemetry. Each company's systems
treat the other's as untrusted inputs, and both are correct to. Neither will
federate with {Aura}.
