### What People Notice First
When Sophia tears the Mind Web and the room sees Kael for the first time:
*A tall woman. Armored in something that is not quite metal and not quite ceramic.
Eyes that are calm in the way that a very old thing that has seen a great deal is
calm — calibrated in a way that human faces are not. She has been standing there
the whole time.*

### The Calibrated Quality
Her expressions are not absent — they are precise. She produces exactly the
expression the situation calls for and nothing adjacent. This reads as uncanny to
humans accustomed to emotional leakage. When she is surprised, it shows as a
pause of about half a second that wouldn't register in a human. When she is
amused, it is a very slight thing at the corners. When she is angry, it is
extremely quiet and very cold.

### The Exception: Food
Kael's composure fails completely on contact with Sol human food. Hot chocolate
produces a visible reaction she cannot suppress. This is the most human she looks
in Arc 2. [AUTHOR INPUT NEEDED] — how does she look when the composure breaks?
Younger? Startled? Delighted?
